## Why This Is a Good Interaction

1. **Led with deal-breaker check.** First thing out of the gate, before any other analysis.
2. **Scored every dimension with specific evidence.** Not "good culture" — actual Glassdoor trends, actual complaint themes, actual numbers.
3. **Didn't give benefit of the doubt on missing data.** DEI page with no numbers = score of 2, not "they're probably working on it."
4. **Called out the ironic gap.** Company helps others measure Scope 3 but hasn't published their own. That's the kind of pointed observation that helps the user.
5. **Gave actionable interview questions** tied to the weak areas.
6. **Honest about risk (VC burn rate)** without being alarmist.
7. **Connected the user's specific background** (oil & gas data engineering) to why this is a good fit — not just generic encouragement.
